Understanding the Multi-Map

The multi-map is a powerful interactive tool designed to visualize resilience data across multiple dimensions. It seamlessly integrates diverse datasets, offering an intuitive interface to explore geographic patterns, trends, and relationships. By presenting layers of information in a unified view, the multi-map enables users to compare spatial data effectively, fostering a deeper understanding of resilience and its influencing factors.

Use the interactive controls to zoom in, explore specific regions, and uncover detailed insights. The multi-map provides valuable context for decision-makers, researchers, and community planners, making it an essential resource for analyzing spatial resilience at various scales.
